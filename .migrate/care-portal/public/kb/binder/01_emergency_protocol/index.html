SECTION 1: EMERGENCY PROTOCOL

OXYGEN-DEPENDENT PATIENT

IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED

Failure to administer oxygen may result in loss of consciousness or death.

  • ADMINISTER OXYGEN IMMEDIATELY
  • Do NOT remove oxygen during transport.
  • Treat hypoxia as primary cause of unresponsiveness.

Respiratory Support Equipment

Located in the "Go Zone" (Main Care Area):

  • Oxygen Tanks (Labeled)
  • CPAP Machine
  • Nebulizer
